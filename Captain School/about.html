The Captain Centre operates as a Centre of Excellence in Education and Research, Sustainability and Sustainable Development Advocacy.


The Centre’s mission is to embark on Social Enterprise projects within its scope of jurisdiction towards Social, Environmental and Economic Dividend


For the past 8 years, the captain schools has been committed to contributing towards poverty alleviating through the provision of high quality free tuition private basic education to Orphans, Children with single parents and the underserved in the Ahanta West Municipality, Western Region Ghana.

Vision

Alleviating Poverty through Free Tuition high quality Private basic Education for the underprivileged. The project is a social enterprise providing Free Tuition private basic education for the lower income group and the underprivileged in the Ahanta West District of the Western Region, Ghana

Focus

• Helping the children in building a firm foundation for their social, emotional and cognitive development.
• To help strengthen the literacy, numeracy and critical thinking skills of the children that enable them succeed in school and life.
